# PRD-001 — Coffee Ordering for Office Floors

## Problem

Office operators need a consolidated morning coffee queue for the
kitchen. Manual chat-based ordering loses items and creates churn.

## Solution

A small web app where operators submit per-floor orders by 09:00; the
kitchen receives a single consolidated queue for the day.

## Infrastructure note

Deploying this requires a Cloudflare zone (DNS + edge cache + a
zero-trust access policy for the kitchen UI). Without the zone the
app cannot be reached at its production hostname.

## Out of scope

Payments, delivery logistics, multi-tenant billing.
