# PRD-001 — Coffee Ordering

## Problem

Customers wait too long at peak hours for ordering and pickup.

## Requirements

- R1: Customer can submit an order from a mobile device.
- R2: Customer receives an order-ready notification.
- R3: Cafe staff can mark orders as picked up.

## Out of scope

- Multi-tenant chain support.
