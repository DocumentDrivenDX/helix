# Product Vision — Coffee Ordering

## Why

Customers waste time waiting in line at peak hours. A mobile-first
ordering experience reduces wait by letting customers order ahead.

## Outcome

Customers receive orders faster; baristas can pace prep against demand
visibility.

## Boundaries

- Single-store v1; chain support is out of scope.
- Mobile web first; native app deferred.
