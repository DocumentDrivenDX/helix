# Product Vision — Coffee-Ordering App

A mobile-first coffee-ordering experience for commuter cafes. Customers
pre-order from a phone, walk in, and pick up without waiting in line.
Initial focus: solo independent cafes (1-3 locations).

## Outcomes

- Reduce average wait time at peak from 8min to <2min for pre-orders.
- Capture 30% of morning rush orders via the app within 6 months of
  launch.

## Non-goals

- Loyalty / rewards (deferred to v2).
- Multi-tenant chain support.
