# Product Vision

## Mission Statement

RecipeHub helps home cooks discover, save, and share recipes with a community of passionate culinary enthusiasts.

## Positioning

For amateur home cooks who spend time searching multiple sources for good recipes, RecipeHub is a social platform that centralizes discovery and community feedback. Unlike recipe blogs with intrusive ads, RecipeHub provides a clean, mobile-first experience focused on what matters: the recipe and the community.

## Vision

A vibrant online community where cooking becomes more social, accessible, and collaborative. Home cooks find inspiration through curated recipes, rate and review dishes they've tried, and confidently share their own creations.

**North Star**: 10,000 monthly active users sharing and discovering recipes with zero friction.

## User Experience

Sarah opens RecipeHub on her phone while at the grocery store. She browses trending recipes, saves a vegetarian pasta dish, checks reviews (4.8 stars), and adds ingredients to her cart. She later posts her own baked cookies with a photo and description. Friends react with likes and comments.

## Target Market

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Who | Amateur home cooks aged 25-55 with mobile-first lifestyle |
| Pain | Scattered recipe sources, unreliable reviews, no community feedback |
| Current Solution | Google, Pinterest, recipe blogs, notes |
| Why They Switch | Single, trusted source with peer reviews and social features |

## Key Value Propositions

| Value Proposition | Customer Benefit |
|-------------------|------------------|
| Centralized recipe discovery | Find new ideas without endless scrolling across sites |
| Peer ratings and reviews | Trust recommendations from real home cooks |
| Mobile-first design | Access recipes anywhere, anytime from any device |
| Community sharing | Share your favorite creations and get feedback |

## Success Definition

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Monthly Active Users | 10,000 by month 12 |
| Recipe Submissions | 500 community recipes by month 6 |
| Average Rating Score | ≥ 4.2 stars across platform |

## Why Now

Home cooks have shifted to mobile devices for meal planning. Existing platforms prioritize ad revenue over usability. Social features for cooking communities are underexploited. The timing is right to build a mobile-first, ad-free alternative.
