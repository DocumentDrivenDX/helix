# Product Vision Prompt

Create a concise product vision that aligns stakeholders on mission, direction, and value.

## Storage Location

Store at: `docs/helix/00-discover/product-vision.md`

## Purpose

A **north star document** that keeps the long-term direction, value, and success criteria clear.

## Key Principles

- **Be concise** - keep the mission to 1-2 sentences.
- **Be specific** - name target customers and measurable success.
- **Be compelling** - connect the vision to customer pain.

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Mission fits in a tweet
- [ ] Vision is ambitious but achievable
- [ ] Target market is specific
- [ ] Value props differentiate from alternatives
- [ ] Success metrics are measurable
