# Go-to-Market Plan Generation Prompt

Create a concise GTM plan for this launch.

## Storage Location

`docs/helix/05-deploy/gtm-plan.md`

## Key Requirements

- Keep the plan service- or product-specific.
- Include only the launch dates, target segments, messaging, channels, metrics, and risks needed to coordinate execution.
- Name owners and measurable outcomes where coordination matters.
- Avoid broad marketing boilerplate or generic channel inventories.

## Completion Criteria

- [ ] Timeline is short and date-driven
- [ ] Segments and messages are specific
- [ ] Metrics and risks are actionable
